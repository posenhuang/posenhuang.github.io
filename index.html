Po-Sen Huang

Hello, and welcome to my website!

I am Po-Sen. I am a researcher at Microsoft Research. My research interests include deep learning, and large-scale machine learning. Before joining MSR, I was a research scientist at Clarifai. I received my PhD from Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), advised by Prof. Mark Hasegawa-Johnson. I received my B.S. degree in Department of Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University, Taiwan in 2008.
