Po-Sen Huang

Hello, and welcome to my website!

I am Po-Sen. I am a staff research scientist at Google DeepMind. Before DeepMind, I worked at Microsoft Research and Clarifai. I received my PhD from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), advised by Prof. Mark Hasegawa-Johnson. I received my B.S. degree from National Taiwan University.
